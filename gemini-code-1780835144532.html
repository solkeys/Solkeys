About The Mentorship

This isn't just about learning songs; it's about understanding music. Over 12 months, we build a foundation that lasts a lifetime.

Months 1-4: The Foundation

Posture, hand position, reading sheet music, and playing your first two-handed melodies.

Months 5-8: Building Fluency

Scales, chords, pedal technique, and early intermediate repertoire.

Months 9-12: The Independent Pianist

Advanced dynamics, sight-reading strategies, and learning how to teach yourself new pieces.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a real piano?
An acoustic piano is great, but a weighted-key digital piano (88 keys) is perfectly fine.

What if I miss a lesson?
We require 24 hours notice for rescheduling, otherwise the lesson is forfeited.
