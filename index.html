Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program suitable for casual learners?

This program is designed for committed students who want structured progress, not casual or occasional learning.

What if I learn slowly compared to others?

The curriculum follows a structured pathway, but your pace is flexible. You progress at a speed that matches your individual development.

How long before I can play songs?

You can expect to start playing simple pieces within the first month. As you continue, you will gradually take on more challenging pieces as your skills develop.

Do I need a real piano?

An acoustic piano is great, but a weighted-key digital piano (88 keys) is perfectly fine.

What do I need for online lessons?

A piano/keyboard, stable internet connection, and a device with camera and microphone.

How does payment work?

Payment is billed in advance on a monthly basis, following a 4-week lesson cycle. Payment details will be provided after your application is accepted.

What if I miss a lesson?

Lesson may be rescheduled with at least 24 hours' notice, subject to availability.
