Most online resources fail because they leave you trapped in an overwhelming maze of random tutorials and conflicting advice, with no structured, step-by-step pathway to lead you toward true mastery. It’s easy to feel like you’ve missed the boat if you didn't start as a child, or to worry that you lack the natural talent to make your hands cooperate. Worse yet, without guidance to evaluate your form, self-taught pianists run the very real danger of unknowingly picking up bad habits. Solkeys Piano Foundations Pathway helps solve these problems. My lessons protect your time and effort by providing a clear, structured pathway designed specifically for minds aged 15 and above. They remove the guesswork, silence the doubts, and ensure that every single minute you spend at the keys builds a flawless foundation—guaranteeing safe, steady progress from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program suitable for casual learners?
This program is designed for committed students who want structured progress, not casual or occasional learning.

What if I learn slowly compared to others?
The curriculum is flexible and adapts to your pace. You are not compared to other students.

How long before I can play songs?
You’ll start playing simple songs from your very first lesson. As you progress, the songs gradually increase in difficulty, introducing new techniques and skills step by step.

Do I need a real piano?
An acoustic piano is great, but a weighted-key digital piano (88 keys) is perfectly fine.

What do I need for online lessons?
A piano/keyboard, stable internet connection, and a device with camera and microphone.

How does payment work?
Payment is made monthly or quarterly before lessons begin.

What if I miss a lesson?
We require 24 hours notice for rescheduling, otherwise the lesson is forfeited.
